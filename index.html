DEM Smart


A smart home feature for people suffering from dementia. Utilizing the Google Calendar API and the Bose Sound touch Control API, a user (specifically family member) can set and trigger specific reminders for various situations throughout a day. The reminder can be recorded by the family member and would then be spoken to the person (through the Bose SoundTouch 10 system) in need during the event.

Built using Google Calendar API, the Bose SoundTouch Control API, HTML, CSS, BootStrap, Python, and a Bose SoundTouch 10 speaker
